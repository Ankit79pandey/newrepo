Angela's Recipe for the best cinnamon

Ingredients

For the dough :

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast.
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup butter

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon.
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the floor and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfet the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
